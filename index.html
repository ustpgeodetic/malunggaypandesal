How to use the map

  1. Find the location of the stall on the map. You can zoom in or use the current location button.
  2. Click “Spotted Malunggay Hot Pandesal Stall”
  3. Click the exact location on the map. A marker will appear and the coordinates will be filled automatically.
  4. Enter the landmark or place name, and the date and time when you saw it.
  5. Submit the report to have it appear on the map and in the recent sightings list.

About

This web-based map was created to support the community especially those who enjoy Malunggay Hot Pandesal by helping them easily locate nearby stalls. Since many vendors are mobile, this platform also aims to make their locations more visible and traceable in real time.

This project was developed by the Department of Geodetic Engineering of USTP CDO in collaboration with USTP AGES YouthMappers and USTP GEARED UP, promoting community-driven mapping and the use of geospatial technologies for everyday needs.
